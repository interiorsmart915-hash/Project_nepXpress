Privacy Policy

Your data is yours. Here's exactly how we handle it.

📅 Last updated: January 1, 2026

NepXpress is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated through your use of our Service:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and business details provided during registration.
  • Shipment Data: Sender and recipient details, pickup and delivery addresses, package dimensions, weight, and declared value.
  • Transaction Data: Payment method details (processed securely via third-party processors), billing address, and transaction history.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, device information, IP address, browser type, and access times.
  • Location Data: With your consent, precise GPS location to enable real-time shipment tracking.
  • Communications: Messages sent to our support team and feedback provided through the platform.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the NepXpress logistics platform and services.
  • Process shipments, generate tracking IDs, dispatch couriers, and confirm deliveries.
  • Send transactional notifications including shipment status updates, delivery confirmations, and alerts.
  • Respond to customer support inquiries and resolve disputes.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and abuse of the platform.
  • Comply with legal obligations under Nepali law and regulations.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve platform performance and user experience.
  • With your explicit consent, send promotional communications about new features or services.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login sessions, security, and core platform functionality. These cannot be disabled without breaking the Service.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the platform so we can improve it. Collected anonymously.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences across sessions.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.

4. Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Delivery Partners: Courier name, contact number, and delivery address are shared with the assigned courier to complete your shipment.
  • Payment Processors: Payment details are processed by PCI-DSS compliant third-party processors. NepXpress does not store full card numbers.
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics, and communications providers who process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by Nepali law, court order, or to protect the safety and security of our users and platform.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2+.
  • Account passwords are hashed using strong cryptographic algorithms and never stored in plain text.
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel with multi-factor authentication.
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing are conducted on our infrastructure.

While we implement strong security practices, no system is completely immune to risk. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

6. Your Rights

As a NepXpress user, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information in your account settings or by contacting us.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time via the unsubscribe link in emails or account settings.
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data, including profiling for marketing purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@nepxpress.com.np. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. Shipment records are retained for a minimum of 5 years to comply with commercial and tax regulations. You may request earlier deletion of non-essential data where legally permissible.

8. Children's Privacy

The NepXpress platform is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes by posting a prominent notice on our platform or by email. Your continued use of the Service after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
