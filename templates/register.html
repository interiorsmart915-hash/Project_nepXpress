About NepXpress
NepXpress is Nepal's leading logistics and delivery management platform built to simplify how packages move across the country. We connect customers, delivery agents, and businesses through a single powerful dashboard. Whether you're shipping a document across Kathmandu or sending a parcel to a remote district, NepXpress ensures your delivery arrives safely, on time, and at a transparent price. Our platform offers real-time shipment management, role-based access for agents and admins, and a seamless experience from booking to delivery confirmation.
